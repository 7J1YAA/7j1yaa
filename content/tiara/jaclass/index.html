--- title: Japanese Amateur Radio Licenses ---

Examination Subjects for Radio Operator's Certificates of the Amateur Radio Service

  1. For the First Class Amateur Radio Operator:
    1. Art of telecommunication (telegraphy)
      Receiving correctly by ear for three minutes of an European plain language text at a speed of 60 characters a minute.
    2. Knowledge of laws and regulations
      Outline of:
      • the Radio Law and the pertinent Orders and Ordinances;
      • the International Telecommunication Convention and its Radio Regulations.
    3. Radio technology
      Outline of:
      • the principles and applications of theory on radio equipment and radio propagation;
      • the principles of radio wave measuring instruments and methods of radio wave measuring;
      • the maintenance and operation of radio equipment.
  2. For the Second Class Amateur Radio Operator:
    1. Art of telecommunication (telegraphy)
      Receiving correctly by ear for two minutes of an European plain language text at a speed of 45 characters a minute.
    2. Knowledge of laws and regulations
      Outline of:
      • the Radio Law and the pertinent Orders and Ordinances;
      • the International Telecommunication Convention and its Radio Regulations.
    3. Radio technology

      4. Basic knowledge of:
      • the principles and applications of theory on radio equipment and radio propagation;
      • the principles of radio wave measuring instruments and methods of radio wave measuring;
      • the maintenance and operation of radio equipment.
  3. For the Third Class Amateur Radio Operator:
    1. Art of telecommunication (telegraphy)
      Receiving correctly by ear for two minutes of an European plain language text at a speed of 25 characters a minute.
    2. Knowledge of laws and regulations
      Brief outline of:
      • the Radio Law and the pertinent Orders and Ordinances;
      • the International Telecommunication Convention and its Radio Regulations.
    3. Radio technology
      Elementary knowledge of:
      • the principles and applications of theory on radio equipment and radio propagation;
      • the principles of radio wave measuring instruments and methods of radio wave measuring;
      • the maintenance and operation of radio equipment.
  4. For the Fourth Class Amateur Radio Operator:
    1. Knowledge of laws and regulations
      Brief outline of:
      • the Radio Law and the pertinent Orders and Ordinance
    2. Radio technology
      Elementary knowledge of:
      • the principles and applications of theory on radio equipment and radio propagation;
      • the maintenance and operation of radio equipment.
